Word To The Mothers

Never underestimate the power of a praying mother/ For her words connect to God and dope addicts recover/ Through the arson of prayer her enemies are scattered/ Though her clothes are tattered/ A potter's vessel; her enemies are shattered/ When they gathered/ Like Long John's shrimp they're battered- Dipped in the sea of the forgotten/ Withered like the fig tree in Mark their fruit is rotten/ She sends up praise/ In the latter days/ Seeking the face of God with a heavenly gaze/ Sweet like a dozen glazed/ She's not fazed/ Though the weapons form God's will she performs/ Trusting in His will, to His word she conforms/ Through the renewing of her mind she is transformed/ Now her garments are adorned (Selah)// The Lord has used her prayers to save and set free many/ Through her works in the kitchen bellies are filled with plenty/ She has exhorted many- Many whom were face down to the ground/ Lost sheep she's found/ Because she is a queen, The Lord Yeshua (Jesus) shall bring her a crown/ To my mother, and all the good mothers! Happy mothers day.

by CJ
